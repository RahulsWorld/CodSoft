About RCB

Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is a franchise cricket team that represents the city of Bangalore in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008, the team is owned by United Spirits, and their home ground is the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium in Bangalore. Virat Kohli (VK) has a strong connection to the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB), having led them as captain for a significant period and remaining loyal to the franchise, even after stepping down from the captaincy role.
